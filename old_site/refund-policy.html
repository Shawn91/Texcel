Refund Policy

Thank you for choosing us for your productivity needs. We offer high-quality software solutions designed to enhance your text processing and data analysis tasks. Our products are available either for free or with a free trial period to ensure you can test their capabilities before making any commitment.

1. Free Products

Our free products are provided at no cost to you and are available for use indefinitely. As these products are offered for free, we do not charge any fees, and therefore, there are no refunds applicable.

2. Products with Free Trial

For our premium products, we offer a free trial period that allows you to evaluate the software's features and functionality before you decide to subscribe or make a purchase. The length of the free trial period is specified for each product at the time of download or registration.

2.1 Trial Period Evaluation

We encourage you to fully evaluate the software during the free trial period to ensure it meets your needs before making a purchase. The trial period is designed to give you a comprehensive understanding of the software's capabilities without any financial commitment.

2.2 No Refunds After Trial Period

Since we provide a free trial period for our premium products, we do not offer refunds once the trial period has ended, and a purchase has been made. We believe the trial period offers a fair and adequate time for our customers to assess the value of our software.

3. Subscription Services

If any of our products are offered on a subscription basis with an initial free trial period, the subscription may be canceled at any time before the trial period expires to avoid any charges. Once the trial period has ended and the subscription fee has been charged, no refunds will be provided.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about our Refund Policy, please feel free to contact us at support@texcel.app.
